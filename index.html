A Spatially Resolved [CII] Survey of Luminous Quasars at z~7

We are performing a comprehensive [CII] survey of quasar host galaxies at zâˆ¼7. The main science goals of this survey are:
1) to characterize star formation activities in the host galaxies of these earliest SMBHs,
2) to measure the dynamical masses of quasar host galaxies,
3) to perform a census of [CII] emitters in the quasar environment, and
4) to probe the cosmic reionization history and quasar life time by combing the [CII] redshift measurements with infrared spectroscopy.
